    Tourism Industry in South Africa

    Tourism industry is a key driver of South Africa economy, as well as a significant source of foreign exchange revenues. The sector contributes about 9% to the country's gross domestic product having tremendous potential for further growth and for the creation of much-needed jobs and employment of citizens.

    The coronavirus pandemic has triggered an unprecedented crisis in the tourism economy. We strongly believe that supporting this strategic sector to rebound has a lot of potential to drive thedomestic economy’s growth.


    Abeona Voyages promotes the responsible tourism with local suppliers and national labour.
